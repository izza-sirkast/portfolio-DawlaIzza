Who is Nikola Tesla?

Nikola Tesla was a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, and a futurist best known for his contribution to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system

Nikola Tesla's Greatest Inventions

  • discovering the alternating current
  • first hydro-electric power plant
  • X-rays
  • Measurement of flux density
  • wireless transmission

A time line of Nikola Tesla's life

  • 1859 - Nikola Tesla is born at Smilijan, Croatia
  • 1875 - Tesla Enrolls In College
  • 1881 - Tesla begin his career as chief electrical engineer
  • 1882 - Tesla goes to work for Edison
  • 1885 - Tesla submits some of his first patents
  • 1887 - Tesla Re-imagines The Electric Motor
  • 1890 - Tesla begins his work on wireless power transmission
  • 1891 - Tesla patents The Tesla Coil
  • 1943 - Nikola Tesla died at age 86, in a Hotel Room in New York
